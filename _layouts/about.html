About

Hi, I’m Eammon Littler. I’m a graduate of Dartmouth College with a Master's in Computer Science. I currently work at Quel Imaging as a software developer, doing research in the world of fluorescence-guided surgery. While I grew up in warm and sunny Southern California, I now reside in White River Junction, Vermont.

I’ve always been a maker, creating marble towers as a kid and playing with 3D printers as an adult. As a maker, the open-source community is very important to me. It’s where I’ve learned a lot of the skills I have today. To give back to the community, I’ve worked towards creating clear guides for every project I post. Currently, my site is filled with 3D printing content. However, I have begun exploring ways to create interactive/useful webpage tools. Figma is my favorite website prototyper and GitHub pages help run my sites easily.

My current interests include 3D design, electronics, cooking, and photography. I’m into fermentation and usually keep a jar of sprouting greens nearby. My sprouts seem healthy, but I'm not as confident about my water kefir...
